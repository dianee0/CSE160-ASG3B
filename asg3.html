Instructions

  • Movement Controls:
    • W: Move Forward
    • S: Move Backward
    • A: Move Left
    • D: Move Right
    • Q: Rotate Left
    • E: Rotate Right
  • Mouse Controls:
    • Hold left mouse button and move to rotate the camera
  • Story:
    • Your bunny ran away from your minecraft home. You found his footprints in the grass, indicating that your bunny entered the maze. Help locate your lost bunny!

Notes to Grader:

These are examples of the bunny being in the maze. The maze is randomly generated, and we find a random open space where the bunny is placed. By refreshing the page, you can see the entrance of the maze changing because the maze is randomly generated. You can hover over the maze and look for the bunny to see that the bunny is randomly placed each time.
